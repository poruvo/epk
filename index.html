Artist Bio

gon pulvo composes and performs experimental electronic music live, crossing hip hop, house, ambient, and jungle. His sets fuse gritty sound design with narrative motifs and reactive visuals, creating immersive performance environments.

His recent work delves into psychological themes through recurring characters and looped dream sequences, unfolding across recorded releases and live shows.

Performance Notes

His recent work delves into psychological themes through recurring characters and looped dream sequences, unfolding across recorded releases and live shows.

With a fully mobile rig, I bring music to life anywhere, anytime—whether it's a pop-up street performance, a private event, or a festival stage. My setup is designed for total flexibility, letting me create immersive sound experiences wherever inspiration or opportunity strikes.

  • Average set length: 15–45 minutes (I can play a full set or a shorter showcase)
  • Extreme Set length: 2-4 hours (I'll need resources for this, but I have the music ready to go.)
  • Rig: M8 tracker, Wind controller (Digital Sax), Tenor & (prolly not) Alto Sax, sampler, live FX; optional audio-reactive GLSL / Synesthesia visuals
  • FFO: experimental bass, cinematic ambient, jungle breaks
